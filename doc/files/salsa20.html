Functions

salsa20.decrypt (key, nonce, ciphertext, rounds)
Decrypt a string.

Parameters:

  • key: A 16-octet or 32-octet bytestring.
  • nonce: An 8-octet bytestring nonce.
  • ciphertext: The encrypted message.
  • rounds: Optional number of rounds. May be 20 (default), 12, or 8.

Return value:

    The unencrypted message.

salsa20.generate (k, v, i, rounds)
Generate the i^th 64-byte block of the Salsa20_k(v) 2^70-byte sequence.

Parameters:

  • k: A 16-byte or 32-byte sequence representing the secret key.
  • v: An 8-byte sequence representing the nonce.
  • i: An 8-byte sequence representing the stream position of the 64-byte block.
  • rounds: Optional number of rounds. May be 20 (default), 12, or 8.

Return value:

    A 64-byte output block.
